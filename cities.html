    Sydney

    New South Wales

    Australia's largest and most popular city, Sydney is known for its stunning harbor, featuring the Sydney Opera House and Sydney Harbour Bridge. Boasting a vibrant cultural scene, beautiful beaches like Bondi, and a lively cosmopolitan atmosphere.

    Canberra

    New South Wales

    As a capital city of Australia, and a city known for it's urban structure, Canberra still offers a blend of natural beauty and modern architecture with its big and spacious parks and monuments like Australian War Memorial.

    Perth

    Western Australia

    Located on the western coast of Australia and known for its beautiful harbor and pristine beaches. With a calmer lifestyle, the city of Perth offers variety of events and outdoor activities making it a prime city for citizens and tourists.

    Melbourne

    New South Wales

    Melbourne is a blend between Sydney and Canberra with it's beautiful harbour and astonishing modern and minimalistic skycrappers. It's also home to one of the most popular tennin tournaments, Australian Open.
